== Description ==

The newly launched Heyzap.com now has a Wordress plugin. Put more than 6000+ of the Internet's most addictive, fun games on your blog.

You get
* Get the power of games: 6000+ of the most addictive casual games
* Super fresh; Get endless, vibrant content updated regularly
* Increase blog traffic and stickiness!

Has a few options
* Add games to your side bar
* Add games to teh end of your blog
* Add embed key to track game plays on your site.

Author URI: http://www.heyzap.com
Plugin URI: http://www.heyzap.com/publishers/wordpress

== Installation ==

1. Unzip the zip file below to your wp-content/plugins directory
2. Active the heyzap widget in the plugin panel on your wordpress dashboard.
3. Find Heyzap Games on your options panel, under the settings tab on the wordpress.org dashboard. Check settings here.
4. (Optionally, to keep track of game plays, get an embed key (http://www.heyzap.com/publishers/new_site) and copy to the text box there.)
5. Go to widgets (shows under Appearence) and add Heyzap Games to your sidebar
